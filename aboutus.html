About Kargolyn Couriers

Who We Are


Kargolyn Couriers is a Kenyan courier and logistics company committed to delivering secure, reliable and timely parcel solutions across the country. We serve individuals, SMEs and corporate clients with professionalism and integrity.


Built on trust, efficiency and accountability, Kargolyn Couriers continues to grow as a dependable logistics partner for businesses that value speed and security.

Our Mission


To provide secure, affordable and innovative courier solutions that exceed customer expectations while maintaining the highest standards of service and professionalism.


Our Vision


To become one of Kenya’s most trusted courier brands through reliability, innovation and customer-focused service.
